# Material Strategy Skill

Use `.agents/skills/t-agent-material-strategy/` when creating or reviewing PPT outlines, HTML reports, executive briefs, product architecture narratives, presales materials, or consulting-style source distillations.

The skill supports four modes:

- business
- product
- presales
- consulting

It should cite `04-sources/ai-dbgpt/` generated indexes when AI_DB_GPT source material is involved.
