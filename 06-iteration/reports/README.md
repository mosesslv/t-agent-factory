# Reports

Use this folder for material intended to be read or presented.

Examples:

- internal report outline
- management brief
- presentation narrative
- share draft
- workshop material

Reports may cite drafts and sources, but accepted product decisions still belong in `05-decisions`, accepted requirements in `01-product/prd`, and accepted eval coverage in `07-evals`.
